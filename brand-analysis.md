## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Audience
Women with ADHD who have already invested in their personal development (therapy, self-help books, podcasts, habits) but still feel stuck. They are intelligent, self-aware, motivated — and frustrated that traditional approaches haven't resolved what's holding them back.

### Psychographic Profile
- Feels "something beneath the surface waiting to shift"
- Has done "the work" but keeps replaying the past
- Experiences overthinking, procrastination, cycles she can't break
- Senses her potential but can't fully access it
- Wants freedom — from old patterns, from systems that never fit her
- Drawn to spiritual and energetic approaches alongside practical coaching
- Likely 30s–50s, career-focused or entrepreneurial, emotionally intelligent

### Pain Points
- Unprocessed trauma and emotions trapped in the body
- Nervous system dysregulation showing up as overthinking, procrastination
- Self-doubt and feeling stuck in cycles
- Having done lots of self-development work with limited deep results
- Being misunderstood — by the world, by systems, by mainstream approaches
- Identity confusion beneath the "ADHD" label

### Aspiration
Sovereignty. To live and work on their own terms. To have their decisions feel clear, their confidence real, and their life expanding — effortlessly.

## 3. Industry & Competitive Landscape

**Primary Industry:** Women's holistic healing, coaching and mentorship  
**Sub-niches:** ADHD coaching · trauma healing · nervous system regulation · energy healing · spiritual coaching

### Modalities Used
- Subconscious clearing
- Energy healing
- Inner child work
- Nervous system regulation
- EFT (Emotional Freedom Technique)
- Intuitive healing
- Life/mindset coaching

### Key Differentiator from Competitors
Most ADHD coaches focus on **managing** symptoms (productivity systems, time management, coping strategies). Jess explicitly rejects this framing. Her positioning is: *ADHD is not something to manage — it is a label sitting on top of deeper wounds. The real work is remembering who you are beneath it.*

This is a significant white-space opportunity. The ADHD coaching market is crowded with functional/productivity coaches. Jess occupies a rarer, deeper lane: **healing-led transformation for ADHD women**.

## 5. Unique Value Propositions

1. **Root cause, not symptom management** — Goes beneath the surface where talk therapy and self-help often stop. Clients explicitly report this as the differentiator ("not got to the root cause of my issues like Jess has").

2. **Lived experience** — Jess is an ADHD woman herself. She lived inside systems that never worked for her. Baby loss became the catalyst for her awakening. This is not academic knowledge — it is embodied wisdom.

3. **Multi-modal healing** — Combines the energetic (energy healing, subconscious work, inner child work) with the practical (coaching, nervous system tools). Few practitioners span both.

4. **Identity-level transformation** — Not about managing ADHD. About helping women *remember who they are* beneath the label, the conditioning, and the overwhelm.

5. **Sovereignty as the outcome** — The end goal is freedom: of time, movement, energy and purpose. Jess models this herself (travelling the world, worldschooling her children).

6. **Global accessibility** — Online-only delivery means no geographic barrier. This is positioned as a feature, not a compromise.

## 7. Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Qualities
- **Intimate** — Speaks directly to "you." Feels like a conversation, not a pitch.
- **Poetic but precise** — Beautiful language that still says something exact.
- **Empowering, not urgent** — Never pressuring. Always inviting.
- **Soulful** — Rooted in spiritual/energetic worldview without being vague or airy.
- **Certain** — Speaks from earned authority, not arrogance.

### Linguistic Patterns
- Short sentences that land with weight: *"This is where the deeper healing work begins."*
- Rhetorical mirroring — phrases that reflect the reader's inner experience back at them
- Second-person throughout ("you," "your")
- Strategic italics on emotionally loaded words (*waiting*, *transformation*, *truth*)
- Em dashes used for rhythm and emphasis
- Gold-highlighted key concepts (sovereignty, fire, expansion)
- "Inner fire" metaphor threaded throughout all service names and copy

### Tone Spectrum
Not clinical · Not corporate · Not coach-bro hustle · Not fluffy spiritual
→ **Grounded feminine wisdom. Sacred but accessible.**

### Words & Phrases in Regular Use
- Inner fire · Sovereignty · Truth · Awakening · Transmuting · Expand
- Root cause · Beneath the surface · Beneath the label
- The work · Deeper healing · Energy body · Subconscious
- Nervous system · Dysregulation · Conditioning · Cycles
- Reclaim · Remember · Shift · Transform
- "The woman you are becoming"
- "Something waiting to shift"
- "You have done so much already"
